Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What sizes of photo prints are available?

All photos can be purchased in 5 different sizes. They are sold in standard paper sizes - the smallest - A3 which is twice the size of the commonly used A4 paper. Each size after that is twice as big as the one before.

  • Size A3 29.7 x 42.0 cms | 11.7 x 16.5 inches
  • Size A2 42.0 x 59.4 cms | 16.5 x 23.4 inches
  • Size A1 59.4 x 84.1 cms | 23.4 x 33.1 inches
  • Size A0 84.1 x 118.9 cms | 33.1 x 46.8 inches
  • Size 2A0 118.9 x 168.2 cms | 46.8 x 66.2 inches

How fast will I get my order?

Most prints are dispatched next working day except bank holidays (Monday-Friday) using Royal Mail 1st Class service - within UK. For overseas deliveries - there is currently offer of a delivery to any EU country with aim to be delivered within 7 working days. Delivery outside EU is not currently offered - apologies for the inconvenience.

What types of payements do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards are accepted.

What paper is used for prints?

Prints are made on high quality photo paper. There are currently no prints on canvas, t-shirts etc. on offer.
